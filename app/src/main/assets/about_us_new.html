STRESS-FREE DINING WITH ALLERYSAFE

Putting your life in someone else’s hands is not something to be taken lightly, but for those who suffer food allergies this is their reality on a regular basis.

It has been said that at least 8% of our population has some form of food allergy and that rate is increasing. For many of those people, eating the wrong food can be fatal. Because of this, some allergy sufferers do not eat out for fear they will not survive their next meal.

We are on a mission to ensure those with allergies can enjoy the same dining experiences as everyone else; so we have created AllergySafe.

AllergySafe is an app that’s free for anyone to download and is a platform for people to review restaurants, cafés, takeaways and bakeries, so those with allergies can eat with peace of mind.

THE STORY BEHIND ALLERGYSAFE- CAM’S STORY

I am anaphylactic to peanuts and all tree nuts.

I have lived with my allergy since I was 13 years old. I used to eat nuts and peanuts all the time, so having to change quickly and at that age was difficult to say the least. My parents in particular were extremely worried about my going into anaphylactic shock when we were eating out as a family.

After living with it for the past 18 years, I have learned to deal with it. However trying new restaurants is difficult for me, and for my long-suffering wife. It can be difficult explaining to people the seriousness of my allergy and have them not take me seriously when I explain, “I WILL die if your food contains nuts or peanuts”. I have been refused service, had people laugh and tell me I would be fine, had food served to me with nuts in it, been served food unsure if I was going to live through it. On occasion I’ve simply walked out of places because I didn’t trust them.

In my early twenties I started travelling through Europe and Asia. This was a real eye-opener for me, trying to find restaurants to eat in or get takeaways from. I ended up in hospital in Florence after I was told the sandwich I had ordered was nut free. I struggled to find restaurants in which I felt confident enough to eat.

I am sure many other people with allergies know how scary it is to trust a person with your life, over and over again. That is why I have started AllergySafe - to highlight to others what restaurants can be relied upon, and to prevent others from repeating my mistake of inadvertently eating a nut sandwich.

HOW ALLERGYSAFE WORKS

The app is designed so you can easily find, via GPS, food outlets near you that have been reviewed by others with allergies. This will give you some peace of mind knowing that your dining choice has systems and processes in place to ensure your needs are catered for.

AllergySafe covers all the bases:

  • Search for places that have the best rankings and reviews, by:

    • Suburbs

    • Distance from you

    • Restaurant name

  • Personalise your account with your own specific needs

  • Follow reviewers with similar tastes and requirements

  • Review your own experiences and rate the service

COMING SOON-NOT JUST FOR DINERS

This app is in its infancy. The intention for the future is that it can be used as an opportunity for restaurant owners and the like to expand their patronage. By improving the level of service offered to those with allergies, you are opening your doors to an entire new community. Each review will be able to be responded to, which avoids one-sided opinions. There will be the opportunity to rectify any negative feedback. Included in the app will be information for food providers to enhance their service to the allergy community, to understand more about allergies, and what sufferers focus on and care about. Our intent is to raise awareness and promote positive change across the whole industry.

COMING SOON-WORLDWIDE REACH

It does not matter if you are eating at your ‘local’ or on the other side of the world. With our translator functionality you can find a restaurant in a foreign country and understand what others have said before you. Start your search by country, city or suburb, and find the best option closest to you, wherever you are in the world.
So many people can benefit from your feedback, now it’s up to you, go out and start reviewing!
